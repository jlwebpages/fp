Submitting Picks

To submit your picks, go to the FORMS page and click Input Form. Put in the password and click Enter. Fill out the form and click Accept. Review your picks and then click Submit via E-Mail. A new e-mail message containing your picks will be displayed. Click Send to e-mail your picks to me. If a new e-mail message isn't displayed, click Copy to E-Mail and carefully follow the instructions. Alternatively, you can submit your picks using an Excel file which can be downloaded from the FORMS page. Click Input Form (Excel) to download the file. Fill out the Excel form and send the file to me as an attachment in an e-mail message.

Preliminary Scores

Go to the FORMS page and click Preliminary. When you enter in game winners (regular season) or game scores (post season) and click Calculate Player Scores, the Preliminary form displays each player's score so you can see who's winning. The Preliminary form will retrieve and display NFL game scores from the internet and calculate player scores when you click Get NFL Scores. To automatically refresh scores, click Auto Refresh: Start.

When you click Best Outcome (regular season), games with winners already specified ("H", "V", or "Tie") will remain unchanged. Each remaining game will be set to "H" or "V" depending on what yields the best outcome for the selected Player. The best outcome is when the selected player ends up first with the biggest difference between his score and the second place score. If the selected player doesn't end up first, then the best outcome is when the difference between his score and the first place score is smallest. If an Opponent is also selected, the best outcome is when the selected player's score is higher than the opponent's score by the biggest difference. If the selected player's score isn't higher than the opponent's score, then the best outcome is when the difference between their scores is smallest.

Final Scores

Go to the FORMS page and click Final. The Final form contains the most recent NFL game results and player scores. Go to the RESULTS page to see a summary of the most recent scores, season average scores, and season winnings for each player. On the FORMS page, click Week 1 through X (where X is the most recent week) to see the overall regular season summary.

Past Season Results

Go to the ARCHIVE page to access past season results.
