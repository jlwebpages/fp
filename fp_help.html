The Input Form - Submitting Your Picks

You must submit your picks by Saturday at 6:00 AM. If there's a Thursday night game, then you must submit your picks by Wednesday at 8:00 PM. Submitting your picks is done by e-mail. So, if you're having problems with your e-mail, then submit your picks to me in person or over the telephone. Either way, you must submit your picks by the stated deadline. If you don't submit your picks on time, you'll receive a score of "0" and you'll still have to pay.

To submit your picks, go to the Football Pool Home Page and click on Forms. Then, click on Input Form. You will be prompted to enter in the input form password. After you have, click on the Enter button. If the password is valid, the input form will be displayed. Enter in all of the required information and click on the Accept button. Your picks will be displayed in window for your review. Click on the OK button to submit your picks.

You can alternately submit your picks using an Excel Form which you can download using the Input Form (Excel) link. To download it, refer to the Downloading Excel Forms section of this help page. After you download it, open it, enter in all of the required information, save it, and send it to me as an attachment in an e-mail message.

The Preliminary and Final Excel Forms

Regular Season:   The Preliminary Excel form allows you to enter the results (H, V, or Tie) in cells E4 through E19 of the Current Week Excel Worksheet. When the results are entered, the scores and rank for each player in row 21 are automatically calculated. The data in row 2 and rows 22 through 27 of the Current Week Excel Worksheet and the entire Season Summary Excel Worksheet are updated in the Final Excel form.

Post Season:   The Preliminary Excel form allows you to enter the final scores of the visiting teams in column B and the final scores of the home teams in column F. After entering the final post season scores, the scores and rank for each player are automatically calculated. The Preliminary Excel form also contains a macro. Depending on how you have your Excel Security set (Tools->Macro->Security...), you may or may not see a window prompting you to enable or disable macros when you open the Preliminary Excel form. If macros are enabled, you can type ctrl-s on the keyboard to sort the tables which are located starting at cell CB39. The Final Excel form is merely a copy of the Preliminary Excel form with all of the data entered for you.

To download the Preliminary and Final Excel form files, refer to the Downloading Excel Forms section of this help page.

Web Page Updates

After 6:00 AM on Saturday morning, the Input and Preliminary Excel forms are updated and available for downloading.

After all of the games are over, the Final Excel form is updated and available for downloading. The Results page is also updated.

Downloading Excel Forms

Follow these instructions to download the Input, Preliminary, and Final Excel forms.

Go to the Football Pool Home Page and click on Forms. Using the mouse, move the cursor over Input Form (Excel), Preliminary (Excel), or Final (Excel) and press the right mouse button. A context menu will be displayed. Click on Save Link As... (Netscape users) or Save Target As... (Internet Explorer users). A Save As window will be displayed which will allow you to select a folder on your local computer. Once you've selected a folder, click on the Save button to download the file to your computer.

In order to open Excel forms, you need to have Microsoft Excel installed on your computer (any version will do). Double-click on the Excel form file to open it. If that doesn't work, then start Microsoft Excel from the Start Menu. Then, from the Microsoft Excel File menu, click on Open.... This will display a window which you can use to find the Excel form file that you downloaded. Select the Excel form file and click on Open.
