Submitting Your Picks

To submit your picks, go to the FORMS page and click Input Form. Put in the password and click Enter. Fill out the input form and click Accept. Your picks will be displayed for review. Click Submit to submit your picks. If the on-line Input Form isn't working for you, you can submit your picks using an Excel file which can be downloaded from the FORMS page. Click Input Form (Excel) to download the file. Fill out the Excel input form and send it to me as an attachment in an e-mail message.

Tracking Games In Progress

Go to the FORMS page and click Preliminary or Preliminary (Excel). When you enter the status of in progress games, the Preliminary form will calculate the scores for each player so you can see who's winning. The on-line Preliminary form can self-populate itself with status of in progress games from the internet. To make this happen, click Get Winners (regular season) or Get Scores (post season).

Current Season Results

Go to the FORMS page and click Final or Final (Excel). The Final form contains the most recent game results and final scores for each player. Go to the RESULTS page to see a summary of the most recent final scores, season average scores, and season winnings for each player. On the FORMS page, click Week 1 through X (where X is the most recent week completed) to see an overall regular season summary.

Past Season Results

Go to the ARCHIVE page to access past season results.
