Submitting Picks

To submit your picks, go to the FORMS page and click Input Form. Put in the password and click Enter. Fill out the form and click Accept. Your picks will be displayed for review. Click Submit to submit your picks. If the on-line Input Form doesn't work for you, you can submit your picks using an Excel file which can be downloaded from the FORMS page. Click Input Form (Excel) to download the file. Fill out the Excel form and send the file to me as an attachment in an e-mail message.

Preliminary Scores

Go to the FORMS page and click Preliminary or Preliminary (Excel). When you enter in winners (regular season) or game scores (post season) and click Calculate Scores, the Preliminary form displays in-progress player scores so you can see who's winning. The on-line Preliminary form will automatically retrieve winners and game scores from the internet when you click Get Winners (regular season) or Get Scores (post season). To refresh winners and game scores automatically every 15 seconds, click Auto Refresh: Start.

Final Scores

Go to the FORMS page and click Final or Final (Excel). The Final form contains the most recent game results and player scores. Go to the RESULTS page to see a summary of the most recent scores, season average scores, and season winnings for each player. On the FORMS page, click Week 1 through X (where X is the most recent week) to see the overall regular season summary.

Past Season Results

Go to the ARCHIVE page to access past season results.
